Overview

First Impressions: Sleek, Light & Surprisingly Premium

The Zebronics Zeb-Blanc is an ultra-slim wireless mouse that arrives looking like it costs twice its price tag. At just 63 grams, it's one of the lightest mice in its class — and you can actually feel that the moment you lift it out of the box.

What struck me first was the multicolor LED underglow. It's subtle, not garish, adding a premium visual flair without screaming "gamer." The matte black finish resists fingerprints surprisingly well, and the overall build quality feels solid despite the lightweight frame.

The detachable nano USB receiver tucks neatly into the bottom panel — a thoughtful travel-friendly design touch that Zebronics often nails in their budget lineup.
